Bio

I received my BSc. in Electronics Engineering with a minor in Mathematics, and MSc. in Electronics Engineering from Sabanci University, Istanbul, Turkey, in 2014 and 2016. I am currently pursuing my Ph.D. in Electrical Engineering at Northeastern University, Boston, MA, USA. My research interests broadly include statistical signal processing and machine learning, with applications to biomedical information processing.

Education:

  • Ph.D., Electrical Engineering, Northeastern University, Boston, MA, USA (2016 - 2020)

      Thesis: "Identifying Neural Correlates of Motor Adaptation Learning for BCI-Assisted Stroke Rehabilitation"
  • BSc., Electronics Engineering (with minor in Mathematics), Sabanci University, Istanbul, Turkey (2010 - 2014)
