Bio

I am currently a postdoctoral university assistant at the Institute of Theoretical Computer Science, TU Graz, Austria. I received my Ph.D. in Electrical Engineering from Northeastern University, Boston, MA, USA. Prior to that, I obtained my BSc. in Electronics Engineering with a minor in Mathematics, and MSc. in Electronics Engineering from Sabanci University, Istanbul, Turkey. My research interests are in the domain of computational neuroscience and biomedical information processing, by using and developing tools for statistical signal processing and machine learning.
